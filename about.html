Hello! I'm Francisca, the founder of Francis, a Barcelona based design practice. My work is set in the realm of visual communication, spanning from branding, web design, editorial design, design for moving image and copywriting for clients of different scales and fields. Here you'll find a selection of projects developed at my current independent practice. I am also the editor and writer of “Graphic Voices”, an initiative of in-depth interviews by ELISAVA to introduce the community behind the MAs in Editorial and Graphic Design.
